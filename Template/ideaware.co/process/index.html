Process

Our process starts with learning and understanding your objectives. Then we create and iterate project phases with you. At the end, you receive a beautiful product with a great user experience.

Doing it the right way

We create websites and mobile apps focusing on content and good user experience. Here’s how we do it:

Research

It all starts by discussing and understanding your business objectives. We then research keeping checks on all of the important variables: competition, target market, users, deadlines
and project expectations.

UX & IA

Perhaps one of the most critical parts of the process: this is where we lay out user flows, interactions, sitemaps and personas to create
a great user experience.

Design

Here is where we shine: we create designs that convey how your website or app is going to look like, send them over and iterate based on team and user feedback until we get it just right.

Development

Once we've nailed the design, our developers will start the process of actually bringing it to life. Using the latest web technologies, we strive hard for fast, clean, and semantic code.

Deploy

We will set up the environment, upload the new website, CMS or mobile app and set everything up for a successful product launch! We’ll even help you out with social media and marketing efforts.
