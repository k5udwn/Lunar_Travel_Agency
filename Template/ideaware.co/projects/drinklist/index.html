Drinklist is an iPhone app we made to solve a simple problem: keep track of your bar tab. We built it for it's practicality and shared it with the world. http://drinklistapp.com

The Problem

You go out with friends for a few drinks. You open up a tab and start ordering your stuff, maybe one in a while you give one to a friend as well. How do you keep tabs on what you've ordered?

That's where drinklist comes in, add drinks, add the price and at the end of your night you know exactly what you need to pay. Don't forget the tip!

The Process

We started out with a problem we found when the team went out for a bit of fun. One night out, we decided to solve it, so we started by creating designs and iterating from there. Once we were happy with those, we built the iOS app in under 2 weeks and uploaded to the app store. The app is a free download for the world do use.
