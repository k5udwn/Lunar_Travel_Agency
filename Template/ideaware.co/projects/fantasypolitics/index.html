We worked with the Fantasy Politics team to create a great user experience and marketing pages for their fantasy-football inspired startup for politics.

The Problem

The Fantasy Politics team came to us with a great idea after they ran into a bit of trouble with their user experience and visual style.

As simple as the rules are for fantasy-football there are a few caveats on how to create a great experience online when related to politics

The Process

We worked with them, tested and iterated designs until we found out the easiest way to play the game online.

Later we applied game mechanics theory to make it not only usable but also fun. We created responsive, html5/css3 markup and delivered to the fantasy politics team.
