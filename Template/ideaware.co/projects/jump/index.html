Jump is an iPhone app for quick on-the-go orders. You can order from your local Starbucks or from all the vendors from a stadium and have it delivered.

The Problem

Our client needed design for for an app they had already built. Basically give it a new face.

There were also some user experience concerns we needed to address to make the app as easy to use as possible.

The Process

Our team worked on creating wireframes and visual designs for the client. We tested a few different interfaces as well as proposed a few visual styles that could work for this type of app.

We also created the landing page for their app.
